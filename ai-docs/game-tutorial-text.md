# Tutorial lesson 1: The basics

Step 1: Select a tile by clicking on it and place it on a green cell.
Step 2: Scroll down and select an entrance.
Step 3: The tile you selected will appear on the green cell you chose and a line will appear on the tile. The line shows the path you took and the end of the line is where you are. If a win screen appears, restart this lesson. It is now Player 2's turn. Repeat steps 1 and 2 for Player 2.
Step 4: If a win screen appears, restart this lesson. To win you have to eliminate everyone on the board or reach your opponent's base. Proceed by placing another tile on a green cell.
Step 5: Lesson complete! Proceed to the next lesson.

# Tutorial lesson 2: Movement and dead zones

Step 1: In this case, the two players are going toward the same cell. Each player must immediately move to the end of the path. Place a centrifugal tile. A win screen should pop up.
Step 2: Player 2 was forced to follow the existing path into a dead zone, marked by an "X". No matter whose turn it is, everyone touching the placed cell is affected by it. Lesson complete! Proceed to the next lesson.

# Tutorial lesson 3: The straightaway tile

Step 1: The straightaway is the most basic tile. Place it and choose the W1 entrance to get to 1.
Step 2: Continue placing straightaways until you get to 2. Place diagonal tile, a centrifugal tile, and another diagonal tile around 2 to get to 3.
Step 3: Continue placing straightaways until you get to 4. Place a centrifugal tile to reach your opponent's base.
Step 4: Lesson complete! Proceed to the next lesson.

# Tutorial lesson 4: The centrifugal and U-turn tiles

Step 1: The centrifugal tile performs a sharp, ninety degree turn. Place it and choose the W0 entrance to get to 1.
Step 2: The U-turn tile curves back to an adjacent entrance of the same edge entered from. Place it down.
Step 3: Continue placing centrifugal and U-turn tiles until you get to 2.
Step 4: Place tiles until you get to 3. Place a weave at 3.
Step 5: Place straightaways until you get to 4. Place a centrifugal tile at 4 to reach the opponent's base.
Step 6: Lesson complete! Proceed to the next lesson.

# Tutorial lesson 5: Diagonals and weaves

Step 1: The diagonal acts as a wider ninety degree turn. Place it and choose the W1 entrance to get to 1.
Step 2: Place a centrifugal tile. Then, place a U-turn 3 times. Then, place a diagonal 3 times. Then, place another U-turn.
Step 3: A weave has an intersection in the center of the tile. Place it to get to 3. Then, place 2 diagonals to get to 2. Place a straightaway at 2.
Step 4: Get to your opponent's base however you want.
Step 5: Lesson complete! Proceed to the next lesson.

# Tutorial lesson 6:

Step 1: Place a centrifugal tile below your base and choose the N1 entrance.
Step 2: Place a diagonal, straightaway, and a centrifugal in their corresponding order.
Step 3: A jump tile "jumps" the cell in front of where the jump tile was placed. Place it to get over the dead zone. You only get two jump tiles in a game.
Step 4: Place a straightaway, a centrifugal tile, another straightaway, another centrifugal tile, then another straightaway, and finally another centrifugal tile.
Step 5: The diagonal jump tile "jumps" to a diagonally adjacent cell. Place it down. You only get two diagonal jump tiles in each game.
Step 6: Place a diagonal, weave, centrifugal, a straightaway, and another weave.
Step 7: Jump tiles can also jump over tiles. Place a diagonal jump tile. Then, place a jump tile.
Step 8: Place a centrifugal tile, a straightaway, a weave, then a diagonal tile.
Step 9: Place 2 centrifugal tiles, a straightaway, and then a weave.
Step 10: Get to your opponent's base however you want.
Step 11: Lesson complete! Proceed to the next lesson.
