# Tile Path Game - Tutorial

## Overview

This document captures the current tutorial flow for the Tile Path Game. It is organized into five lessons, with step-by-step instructions and preset notes where needed.

---

## Tutorial Lesson 1: The Basics

1. Select a tile by clicking on it and place it on a green cell.
2. Scroll down and select an entrance.
3. The tile you selected will appear on the green cell you chose and a line will appear on the tile. The line shows the path you took, and the end of the line is where you are. If a win screen appears, restart this lesson. It is now Player 2's turn. Repeat step 1 for Player 2.
4. If a win screen appears, restart this lesson. To win, you have to eliminate everyone on the board or reach your opponent's base. Proceed by placing another tile on a green cell.
5. Lesson complete. Proceed to the next lesson.

## Tutorial Lesson 3: The Straightaway Tile

Preset: always skip Player 2's turn. Use the second reference image.

![Tutorial Lesson 3 Preset](/home/xcstar/.cursor/projects/home-xcstar-xcstarz-github-io/assets/Screenshot_20260307_173616-1336eb58-54ab-4877-bbb4-6c282079d4b0.png)

1. The straightaway is the most basic tile. Place it and choose the `W1` entrance to get to `1`.
2. Continue placing straightaways until you get to `2`. Place a diagonal tile, a centrifugal tile, and another diagonal tile around `2` to get to `3`.
3. Continue placing straightaways until you get to `4`. Place a centrifugal tile to reach your opponent's base.
4. Lesson complete. Proceed to the next lesson.

---

## Tutorial Lesson 4: The Centrifugal and U-Turn Tiles

Preset: same as Lesson 3.

1. The centrifugal tile performs a sharp, ninety degree turn. Place it and choose the `W0` entrance to get to `1`.
2. The U-turn tile curves back to an adjacent entrance of the same edge entered from. Place it down.
3. Continue placing centrifugal and U-turn tiles until you get to `2`.
4. Place tiles until you get to `3`. Place a weave at `3`.
5. Place straightaways until you get to `4`. Place a centrifugal tile at `4` to reach the opponent's base.
6. Lesson complete. Proceed to the next lesson.

---

## Tutorial Lesson 5: Diagonals and Weaves

Preset: same as Lesson 3.

1. The diagonal acts as a wider ninety degree turn. Place it and choose the `W1` entrance to get to `1`.
2. Place a centrifugal tile. Then place a U-turn 3 times. Then place a diagonal 3 times. Then place another U-turn.
3. A weave has an intersection in the center of the tile. Place it to get to `3`. Then place 2 diagonals to get to `2`. Place a straightaway at `2`.
4. Get to your opponent's base however you want.
5. Lesson complete. Proceed to the next lesson.
